About TECHY ME
TECHY ME is a gamified learning platform that helps students master IT skills through interactive modules and games.

Tailored for beginners and high school learners, our modules cover:
  • ⚙️ Networking – Learn wiring and connections
  • 💻 Programming – Practice HTML, CSS, JS, PHP
  • 🧩 System Unit – Assemble/disassemble PC parts

What makes TECHY ME different?
TECHY ME is not just educational — it's interactive, competitive, and user-friendly. It combines visual challenges, problem-solving exercises, and step-by-step tutorials to make learning both efficient and exciting.

Key Features:
  • 🧠 Unlockable difficulty levels and achievements
  • 📱 Fully responsive for desktop, tablet, and mobile
Benefits of Using TECHY ME:
  • ✅ Learn core IT skills in a structured and fun environment
  • ✅ Improve logical thinking and practical problem-solving
  • ✅ Build foundational knowledge for future tech careers
  • ✅ Encourage self-paced, goal-driven learning
Whether you're new to tech or just want to improve your skills, TECHY ME gives you the tools, challenges, and motivation you need to succeed.
